### Text
Text searching predicates on the following attributes of stamps:
- `Session - Title`
- `Flag Stamp - Title`
- `Note Stamp - Label`
- `Cue Stamp - Label`
- `Eos Stamp - Label`
- `Disguise Stamp - Section Name`
